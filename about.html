About the Artist

Beth Sutton

Artist, Designer, and Muralist

Beth has been an artist and mural painter since she was young. She was first commisioned back in 2003 by her mother to paint a mural on the wall at the end of the hall. Since then, Beth has painted murals all across Colorado.

In her spare time, Beth is also a professional software engineer and certified positive-reinforcement dog trainer.
