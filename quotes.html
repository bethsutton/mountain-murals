Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a mural take?
In general, one wall in a room takes about 2-5 full days of painting, depending on the complexity of the design. A mural of 20 sq ft can usually be completed in just one day.
How much does a mural cost?
Each mural is a custom piece, and is therefore unique. We will submit a quote for each client after our initial consultation.
Do you paint the base coat?
Yes! While we do request our clients provide clean walls free of personal items or wall decoration, painting the base coat is included in our services.
What paint do you use?
We use Benjamin Moore in eggshell finish.
I have my own design, can you paint it for me?
In most cases, yes! While the finished mural will have our own style, we can usually recreate a design faithfully.
How do I clean a finished mural?
We use a mess-friendly eggshell interior latex paints, which means that marks or dirt can usually be washed off with gentle non-abrasive cleanser.
